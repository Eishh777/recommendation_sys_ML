Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Flask and Machine Learning

In the age of technology and information, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more critical than ever...

The Problem

People often experience symptoms and want quick answers about their health concerns. Our goal was to provide a convenient solution for users to input their symptoms and receive potential diagnoses.

The Solution

We developed a web app that allows users to enter a list of symptoms and predicts the most likely disease using a pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier model.

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter symptoms through a user-friendly interface.
  2. Machine Learning Model: Trained with a dataset containing symptoms and corresponding diseases.
  3. Prediction: The model predicts the most likely disease instantly.
  4. Additional Information: Includes disease descriptions, precautions, medications, dietary advice, and workouts.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive and easy to navigate.
  • Accurate Predictions: Uses machine learning for precise results.
  • Comprehensive Information: Disease descriptions, precautions, medications, and more.
  • Educational Content: Includes informative blog posts on health topics.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App brings machine learning and healthcare together, making healthcare information accessible and user-centric. We hope this project contributes to better health awareness and improves overall well-being.
